Meet Coleen

Coleen is passionate about her succulent collection and enjoys expanding her collection. What started as a mere hobby grew into a passion that has become a source of enjoyment for Coleen. Having just retired and coping with cancer, she finds caring for her garden uplifting as it helps to relieve stress.

The Store

Coleen’s Garden is a growing online store in Hawaii that specializes in selling succulents. We have a wide variety of succulents to choose from at affordable prices shipped with aloha.

What's the Big Deal?

Not only are succulents beautiful to look at, they are also relatively easy to grow and maintain. With such a wide variety of succulent out there, there’s sure to be something for everyone. Plus, succulents can be grown from just a single petal. These plants are perfect for busy people on the go as well as the avid plant-lover or those interested in keeping plants.
